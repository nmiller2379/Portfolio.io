About me

I am a full stack web developer with a background in journalism and a life-long dedication to learning, teaching and innovating.

My colleagues know me as a self-starter who is a strong multi-tasker and good teammate with wit, sense of humor and attention to detail.

Recently, I earned a certificate in full stack web development from the University of Denver, developing skills in JavaScript, CSS, React.js, and responsive web design.

I worked on a team of three to develop a full-stack web application that helped a team member solve a real-world problem his company faced.

I was born in Ohio, but grew up in Denver, attending Denver South High School before moving to Greeley to attend the University of Northern Colorado. After I left the Tribune, I returned to Denver to be closer to family.

In my free time, I am a history buff who also enjoys sports, cooking and spending time with friends and family.
